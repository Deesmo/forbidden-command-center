📖 Command Center Guide

📸 Photo Gallery

Browse all 35+ bottle product photos at /photos. Photos are organized by category — product shots, lifestyle, single barrel, small batch. Click any photo to view full-size, copy its URL, copy an HTML img tag, or use it in a new post.

⭐ Photos marked "Hero" have clean light backgrounds — best for AI image reference and blog headers.

✏️ Compose

Create social media posts with AI assistance. Choose a tone (Professional, Casual, Playful, Educational, Luxury), select target platforms, and attach photos from the gallery. AI Generate creates platform-optimized content using Claude or GPT.

📝 Blog Hub

Generate SEO-optimized blog articles about bourbon topics. 57 pre-loaded topics across categories. Articles auto-include a featured bottle photo. Publish to Medium, WordPress, Blogger, or Reddit with one tap.

Strategy: 2-3 articles per week across all platforms for maximum SEO impact and backlinks to drinkforbidden.com.

🎨 AI Studio

Generate AI images using GPT-Image-1.5 (with your real bottle as reference). Style presets: Product, Lifestyle, Art Deco, Cocktail, Social Ad, Editorial. Generate AI videos with Runway ML.

When "Use Bottle Reference" is checked, the AI uses your actual Forbidden Bourbon bottle photo (Black_Front_LightBG_V1.png) to ensure brand consistency.

🔍 Brand Intelligence

Scan the web for mentions of Forbidden Bourbon — reviews, features, press, awards, podcasts, videos, events, and social mentions. Star important mentions and fetch full content.

Note: Brand Intel only tracks mentions OF Forbidden Bourbon — never retail/store listings.

📬 Outreach Hub

CRM for bourbon influencer outreach. Scan finds 35+ bourbon YouTubers, bloggers, Instagram creators, podcast hosts. Track status from "new" through "posted about us". Copy pre-built email templates for influencers, bars, and press.

📡 Platforms

Connect your social accounts and API keys. Set these in Render environment variables:

ANTHROPIC_API_KEY OPENAI_API_KEY RUNWAY_API_KEY MEDIUM_TOKEN WORDPRESS_SITE WORDPRESS_TOKEN BLOGGER_BLOG_ID

📊 Mash Analytics

Sales data from your Mash Networks e-commerce portal. Upload screenshots of Demographics, Geography, Customer, Products, Marketing, and Baskets tabs to populate the dashboard.
